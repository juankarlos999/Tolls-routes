About app

The platform allows calculating the travel costs of a cargo vehicle.
For example: if the service originates in Bogota and ends in Cartagena,
the value of the route must be charged to the system, including the
following costs: tolls, gasoline value, vehicle wear and tear, etc.
Route and costs is also a (Api Rest) service that performs an approximate
calculation of the expenses of a certain cargo vehicle on a define route.
